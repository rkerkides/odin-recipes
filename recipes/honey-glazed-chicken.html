Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This honey chicken recipe was given to me by a friend. I've used this recipe for years, and my family requests it often. It's a light meal that everyone will enjoy. Serve with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade honey glazed chicken:

  1. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides, about 6-8 minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium-low and pour the honey mixture over the chicken.
  5. Stir to coat the chicken evenly with the honey glaze.
  6. Cook the chicken for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the glaze thickens and the chicken is fully cooked.
  7. Remove the skillet from heat and let the chicken rest for a few minutes to allow the glaze to set.
  8. Serve the honey glazed chicken over rice or with your choice of side dishes.
